SITUATION 01
The Monday
How this works
01
You are the Project Manager on a live rollout. Messages will arrive in your inbox. People need things from you. Read, decide, move.
02
There are no right answers. Every choice has consequences that show up in the project state panel on the right. Watch how they move.
03
You will be interrupted once. At one point, time stops. You will be asked to step back and name what the situation is actually about.
04
Your run produces a review and a playbook entry. After about four decisions, the scenario closes and replays your choices as patterns you can carry into real projects.
Five to seven minutes. Treat it like a live project. There is no practice mode.
